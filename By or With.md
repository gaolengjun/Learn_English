❓哪一个是正确的？

> [!question] 问题1
> 1. I learn English by watching ENGVID. 
> 2. I learn English with watching ENGVID.

>正确答案：1

> [!question] 问题2
> 1. I write with a pen.
> 2. I write by a pen.

> 正确答案：1

## By
1. I turn on the computer **by** pushing the on button.
2. I keep healthy **by** exercising.
> **By** 后面跟**动词**，且是一个**ing的动词**，它表示**通过动作完成某事**。
### How do you study?
### I study **by** ……
> reading over my notes
### By还可以用于沟通方式、交通工具，后面直接跟名词

> [!example] 例子
> 1. I will contact you **by** email.
> 2. I will message you **by** text.
> 3. I will go by **by** bus.
> 4. I came **by** car.

❗用 *by* 修饰名词时，名词前*不需加冠词*

---
## With
>**with** 后面通常跟**名词**，它表示**借助某个东西做某事**，可以是**身体部位、工具和器械**

> [!example] 例子
> 1. Italians talk **with** their hands.
> 2. I point **with** my fingers.
> 3. I cleaned the floor **with** a mop.
> 4. I ate dinner **with** a fork.
