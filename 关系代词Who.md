## 什么是关系代词
1. Emma is a teacher.
2. Emma likes flowers.
合并：Emma is a teacher **who** likes flowers.
	Who 为关系代词，代替人称，减少重复人称，将两个概念合并为一个句子，使句子更有张力。
## 更多示例
>I have a friend. 
>He loves movies.
>使用关系代词who代替a friend
>>I have a friend **who** loves movies.

>She has a dog. 
>The dog drools a lot.
>who 代替 the dog。"drools" 是一个动词，意思是“流口水”。
>>She has a dog **who** drools a lot.

>I have a brother. 
>My brother has a cat.
>who 代替brother，减少重复，合并句子
>>I have a brother **who** has a cat.

> My friend is a cop. 
> He is strong.
> Who 在这里不仅代替My friend，还起到了限定的作用
> My friend **who** is strong is a cop.
> 也可以写成：My friend is a cop **who** is strong.

> I met a  nice woman.
> She helped me learn English.
> I met a nice woman **who** helped me learn English.
