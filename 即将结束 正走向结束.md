1. The performance is coming to an end. So, let's give them a big round of applause. 表演即将结束。所以，让我们给他们热烈的掌声。
    
2. The contract is coming to an end. So, we need to discuss renewal options. 合同即将到期。所以，我们需要讨论续约选项。
    
3. The semester is coming to an end. So, students are busy preparing for exams. 学期即将结束。所以，学生们正忙于准备考试。
    
4. The movie is coming to an end. So, the audience started to gather their belongings. 电影即将结束。所以，观众开始收拾他们的随身物品。
    
5. The long journey is coming to an end. So, we can finally rest. 漫长的旅程即将结束。所以，我们终于可以休息了。

>[!important]
>“an end”是一个名词短语，意思是“一个结束”。 “be coming to an end”是一个动词短语，意思是“正在走向结束”。
